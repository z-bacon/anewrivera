FAQ

Answers to questions you may have

Is there an RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by February 14, 2026 via our website or by texting Shaira at (408) 881-4877. We unfortunately can't accommodate RSVPs past this date.

Where will the wedding be?

All wedding events will be at Arbor Terrace at Grand Tradition Estate and Gardens. Be sure to turn left and follow signs for Arbor Terrace once you enter.

What time Should I Arrive

Guests should arrive at 2:30 PM for welcome drinks and seating. The ceremony will begin at 3 PM sharp and we can't accommodate late arrivals.

What should I wear?

The dress code is cocktail attire. This generally means that knee-length or midi cocktail dresses, evening dresses, sundresses, jumpsuits, casual suits, slacks, button-downs, suits and sports coats with or without ties are perfect for our wedding.

The ceremony and cocktail hour will be fully outdoors, and reception will be in a partially outdoor space, so we recommend bringing a layer! It's typically X in Fallbrook in March.

While we appreciate everyone's individual style, we kindly request that guests refrain from wearing informal clothing like shirts and jeans. And in case you were curious, the wedding party will be wearing lavender.

Will there be parking at the venue?

There's lots of parking available at the venue! If needed, you can leave your vehicle at the venue overnight, as long as it's picked up by noon on Sunday.

Will there be transportation provided?

If you choose not to drive to the venue or plan to drink (it is an open bar after all), we're sponsoring Uber rides to and from the venue. We'll send you codes the day of!

Can I bring a Plus One / Date? Can (another person) come?

The number of seats and names of guests in your party are listed on your invitation. Your invitation will be made out to {Your name} and Guest if a plus one has been added to your party. Unfortunately, we can't accommodate any additional guests.

Can I bring my kids?

We love your little ones, but we have decided to have our wedding be mostly child-free, with the exception of a few select family members. Unless noted on your invite, we kindly ask that only guests aged 12 and up attend.
